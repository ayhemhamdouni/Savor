Rooted in heritage, crafted with passion

Founded in 2019, Savor brings the warmth of Tunisian hospitality to a contemporary setting. Every dish tells a story of generations-old recipes refined through modern technique.

Our chefs source ingredients from local farms and spice markets, ensuring authenticity in every bite. From our signature couscous to our house-made harissa, we honor tradition while embracing innovation.
